Analysis

This exercise yielded surprising results. I really didn't expect the participant's sorts to differ from mine very much. It was especially interesting to see the way my sort (#1) differed from the other sorts. Because I had to view the site in order to write down the content, I was biased in developing groups and organization. It was pleasing to see that the other sorts differed. All of the sorts had some sort of "nasa and the government" and an "about the moon" section. Two of the sorts were realy helpful in showing what type of content a user might expect to be on the home page.
